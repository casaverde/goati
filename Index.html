About Us

We are a team of data scientists and AI engineers so passionate about (indoor) racket sports. Our focus on these sports - especially table tennis - is inspired by the speed, spin and precision demand of modern competitions and the limited coverage of traditional coaching methodologies (especially at the non-professional levels) which unaided tantamounts to foreclosing the opportunities to realizing the champion self of teeming enthusiasts. With technology, students will self-assess, get plans, execute and gain feedback (AI/Physical) in cycles while marking milestones towards actuating the champion self.

Our Mission

We are committed to assisting enthusiasts/players of racket sports realizing their champion selves through application of modern technology - especially AI. Regardless of age or stature we will partner with you to actualize your set goals - including getting you a slot on the podium.

Come let's build a winning strategy around your unique playing style! We hope you know you rock.
